# Thind Transport Recruitment Video Script & Research

## ðŸ“Š Research: What Top Trucking Companies Do in Recruitment Videos

### Industry Analysis - Key Findings

**1. Successful Recruitment Video Patterns:**
- **Hook in First 3 Seconds**: Top performers grab attention immediately with a bold statement, question, or visual
- **Real Driver Testimonials**: Authentic voices outperform scripted actors by 3x in engagement
- **Specific Numbers**: "91% commission" beats "high pay" - concrete numbers build trust
- **Problem-Solution Framework**: Address driver pain points (forced dispatch, hidden fees, broken promises) then show how you solve them
- **Visual Storytelling**: Show trucks, drivers, dispatch center - not just talking heads
- **Multiple Lengths**: Create 15s, 30s, 60s, and 2-3min versions for different platforms

**2. What Makes Drivers Listen & Want to Join:**

**Emotional Triggers:**
- **Respect**: "You're not just a number" - drivers feel undervalued at mega carriers
- **Financial Freedom**: Show real earnings, not promises - "$35K more per year" resonates
- **Control**: "You choose your loads" - autonomy matters more than many realize
- **Family/Home Time**: "We honor your schedule" - missed milestones are real pain points
- **Transparency**: "No hidden fees" - drivers are skeptical after bad experiences

**Trust Builders:**
- Founder/owner speaking directly (not corporate spokesperson)
- Real driver testimonials with names and locations
- Specific safety ratings and certifications (A+ rating, FMCSA certified)
- Company history and growth story
- Behind-the-scenes footage (dispatch, maintenance, office)

**3. Platform-Specific Strategies:**

**Website (2-3 minutes):**
- Full story, company values, detailed benefits
- Multiple CTAs throughout
- Can be more detailed and comprehensive

**Social Media (15-60 seconds):**
- Hook-driven, fast-paced
- Single clear message
- Strong visual first 3 seconds
- Platform-optimized (vertical for TikTok/Reels, horizontal for YouTube/Facebook)

**YouTube (2-5 minutes):**
- Can be longer form
- Include detailed testimonials
- Behind-the-scenes content
- SEO-optimized titles and descriptions

### **What to Film/Include:**

**MUST-HAVES:**
1. **Founder/Spokesperson Speaking**: Direct to camera, authentic, confident

1. **Behind-the-scenes**: Maintenance bay, office, team meetings
2. **Real Driver Testimonials**: Video testimonials if possible
3. **Settlement Statements**: Blurred but visible (with permission)
4. **Broker Logos**: Amazon, Walmart, etc. (if permission granted)
5. **Safety Certifications**: A+ rating badge, FMCSA certification

### **Visual Style:**
- **Color Palette**: Navy blue, orange (company colors)
- **Tone**: Professional but approachable, not overly corporate
- **Editing**: Fast-paced cuts for social, slower for website
- **Text Overlays**: Bold, clear, easy to read on mobile
- **Music**: Upbeat but not distracting, conveys movement/progress
